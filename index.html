Welcome to BELLY Budget!

Why BELLY Budget?

Do you often ask yourself am I working out enough to work off what I just ate? How many calorie is this slice of cake? How long should I be on the treadmill to work off 400 calories? If you find yourself asking those type of questions then this application is for you. Simply type in the type of food you're about to eat (or thinking of eating) to see its calorie count. Stop there or continue to see recommendations on the type of exercises and how long you should be doing them to burn off the unwanted calories.

This application is perfect for those who:

  • 💪 Count calories
  • 💪 Have a desire to loose weight
  • 💪 Wish to maintain a healthy lifestyle
  • 💪 Are just curious

Food Page Instructions

  1. Type all the foods and servings you want to eat into the search bar
  2. Click the "Submit" button find out the calories and other nutrition facts
  3. Checkout the nutrition label on the left for the total count



Exercise Page Instructions

  1. Enter your age, weight, height, and gender
  2. Select the type of exercise you wish to do from the drop down
  3. Click "Submit" to see various options on how long you need to exercise in order to burn your desired amount of calories
